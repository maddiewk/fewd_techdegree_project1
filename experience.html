Experience

My music and coding experience

Music

I began my music studies when I was six. My mother was a pianist and she taught me piano at home all the way until I graduated high school. I began learning the violin when I was eight years old and I loved it so much that it became my main instrument.

When I started to get serious about having a career in music, I began entering local and state competitions, performing regularly at different venues, and participating in many different youth orchestras. I received my Bachelor's degree in violin performance at the University of Maryland in 2013.

Coding

I began my coding journey in the winter of 2017. It started as a curiosity of what the industry was all about, since my father is a software developer. After I learned a little bit of HTML, my interest was piqued and I wanted to learn even more. So when I stumbled upon Treehouse, I knew that I wanted to sign up for one of the Techdegree tracks.
